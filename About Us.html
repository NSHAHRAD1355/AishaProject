History of the company

Through a personal coliving experience, Aisha, our founder and CEO, rented a room in a house owned by a recent divorcee with a 3-year-old daughter. As a single mom, she needed extra income to help support the two of them. Aisha found the two of them to be wonderful companions. A special bond formed between them, which made coliving both beneficial and a great experience, leaving them with fond memories. Aisha believes coliving can be an excellent opportunity for wellness living which is beneficial to all. But done wrong, coliving can have adverse outcomes. This journey led Aisha to build a platform that provides women with the benefits of shared accommodation. She believes that women can add a lot of value to each other in support, comfort, and inspiration while navigating the stress and demands placed upon them by society. women homeowner hugging a tenant

Our vision

What Aisha Real Estate Development Inc. aims to do is to establish a development which is an aggregation of these cohousing communities. So if we have an aggregation of cohousing communities we will have a larger community of 1000 seniors. Regardless of the number of seniors in each cohousing community and the number of cohousing communities within the aggregation, the idea is to develop a structure where each cohousing community acts as a nucleus within this structure.

Our mission

Aisha Comfortable Coliving spearheads coliving services to women and property owners in Nova Scotia with a pioneering spirit. We provide affordable coliving to women and community-building activities for our coliving members, and in addition, we provide property maintenance services through our platform. We offer the opportunity for property investors to expand their residential portfolio into the new coliving trend in Nova Scotia.

OUR TEAM

Meet the Founder & CEO of Aisha Comfortable Coliving

Aisha came to Canada as a graduate student where she attended the Memorial University of Newfoundland, completing a Masters in Computational Science and a PhD in Quantum Chemistry. With a background focused primarily in Chemistry and Computer Science, Aisha unexpectedly found her passion for helping others through a personal coliving experience. Aisha is now dedicated to creating a community of women able to experience the same joy and safety of coliving through her very own platform, Aisha Comfortable Coliving.
