Community-building Activities

A community of one hundred seniors is large enough for seniors to socialize but not too large where they dont get to know each other and bond together.What Aisha Real Estate Development Inc. aims to do is to establish a development which is an aggregation of these cohousing communities.

Senior Health Activities

there could be a common building that serves all cohousing communities such as gym with training programs developed specifically for seniors

Medical Care

Understanding the needs of the seniors we target will allow them to age in place and use the platform I am suggesting to its full potential. Medical center where geriatricians are the primary care doctors.

Dancing Studio

Dance, like any form of exercise, benefits seniors in many ways. Regular physical, mental, and social stimulation is the gold standard recommendation for reducing the risk of dementia. Because it involves all three, dancing can be seen as a “triple-threat” option for older adults who want to protect their brains.
